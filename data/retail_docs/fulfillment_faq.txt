Retail Fulfillment FAQs

Q: What happens if inventory is out of stock?
A: The order is placed on backorder and customers are notified. If restock takes longer than 7 days, support offers alternatives or cancellation.

Q: How are delivery delays handled?
A: If carrier delays exceed 48 hours, the order status changes to delayed and an escalation ticket is created for customer outreach.

Q: What is the standard delivery window?
A: Standard delivery is 3-5 business days for in-stock items.

Q: When should an issue be escalated?
A: Escalate when an order is delayed, lost, or exceeds the promised delivery window by more than 2 days.
